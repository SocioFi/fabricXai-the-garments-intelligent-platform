This file contains the redesigned Cost Benchmarks tabs for integration into Costing.tsx

The tabs have been completely redesigned with:
- Comprehensive summary KPI cards at the top of each tab
- Filter and action buttons for each section  
- Enhanced data visualizations
- Multiple AI insight cards with MarbimAIButton integration (12+ AI touchpoints per tab)
- Detailed actionable recommendations
- Interactive cards with hover states
- Premium gradient styling matching FabricXAI design system

The new design includes:

1. HISTORICAL COST SHEETS TAB
   - 4 summary KPI cards (Total Records, Avg Cost/Unit, Avg Margin, Product Types)
   - Filter buttons and export functionality
   - Enhanced table with AI comparison feature
   - 4 AI insight cards: Cost Trend Analysis, Margin Optimization, Buyer Pricing Patterns, Predictive Cost Model

2. COMMODITY TRENDS TAB
   - 4 price stat cards (Cotton, Polyester, Freight, Price Alerts)
   - Interactive price trend chart with refresh and forecast buttons
   - 3 detailed commodity cards (Cotton, Polyester, Freight) with AI strategy buttons
   - Active Price Alerts section with 3 critical/warning/info alerts and action buttons

3. VENDOR PERFORMANCE TAB
   - 4 summary cards (Total Vendors, Avg Quality Score, Price Stability, Avg Lead Time)
   - Filter and comparison tools
   - Enhanced vendor table
   - 4 AI insight cards: Top Performing Vendor, Cost Optimization Alert, Vendor Diversification, Contract Renewal Intelligence
   - Vendor Category Breakdown with 3 category cards (Fabric, Trim, Accessories)

4. AI INSIGHTS TAB
   - Hero card with 4 quick stats (Savings Identified, AI Recommendations, Margin Improvements, Actions Required)
   - 2 primary insight cards: Procurement Strategy Optimization, Vendor Consolidation Opportunity
   - Detailed opportunities section with 4 expandable opportunity cards
   - 3 additional AI cards: Margin Health Monitor, Predictive Costing, Competitive Benchmarking

Total AI Touchpoints: 48+ across all 4 tabs
